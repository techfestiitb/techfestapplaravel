About Us

Techfest, IIT Bombay invites you to the 4th edition of the Techfest Cyclothon. Experience the serenity of nature in the heart of the urban jungle as you pedal across a 12 km long route that will provide you with a glimpse of the natural beauty of the lush, green IIT Bombay campus.
Join us on the 29th of September in pledging to include this eco-friendly mode of transport in our daily lives and take the first step towards stopping air pollution with the Techfest Cyclothon!

Vision

With a vision to promote cycling as a green and eco-friendly means of transport, the Techfest Cyclothon aims to help you take the first step in the fight against air pollution by adopting the humble bicycle as the perfect alternative to fuel guzzlers in cities like Mumbai.

History

The Techfest Cyclothon was initiated in 2016 with Techfest’s social awareness campaign called ReCycle to promote greener environment and fitness among the youth. Since then, the Cyclothon has become an annual occurence. In 2017, Techfest, IIT Bombay conducted the 2nd edition of the Cyclothon promoting awareness about heart diseases and how cycling and exercise prevent it. In 2018, the latest edition of the Techfest Cyclothon was organized with the motto: “Balance life on wheels” to break-off the inception of stress and depressed thoughts by primarily promoting a healthy mind.
